Instructions

To help you in the task, your log book will record your experiences with each mushroom you eat. Whenever you eat or avoid a mushroom, a circle will be added to log above the species.

If you eat a healthy mushroom a green circle will appear, and if you eat a poisonous mushroom a red circle will appear. The observation circles are sorted by type, with gray circles first, then red, then green.

If you avoid eating a mushroom, a gray circle like the ones you will observe in the first phase will appear.

Important: A record of your past decisions to eat or not eat mushrooms is recorded in your log (red=poison,green=healthy, and gray=avoided).
