Instructions

In the second phase, when you see a mushroom press "eat" to eat the mushroom or "don't eat" to avoid the mushroom.

In the upper left corner of the screen you will see your current energy level. Your energy goes up by five (5) points when you eat a healthy mushroom, and down by five (-5) when you eat an unhealthy mushroom.

When you avoid a mushroom your energy level won't change, and you won't find out whether it was poisonous or not.

Important: Eating a mushroom that ends up healthy increases you energy. Eating a poisonous mushroom decreases your energy. Avoiding has no effect on your energy.
