Instructions - Payment and Bonus

When you eat a mushroom that is healthy, you will earn five cents ($0.05). When you eat a mushroom that is poisonous, you will lose five cents (-$0.05). If you decide to avoid a mushroom, you won't increase or decrease your winnings.

To maximize your winnings, you should try to eat as many healthy mushrooms as you can, while trying to avoid poisonous mushrooms. You will begin the hike back with 50 cents ($0.50).

At the end of the experiment, one of the ecosystems you explore will be chosen at random. Your bonus will equal your winnings in that ecosystem.

Important: Eating a mushroom that is healthy increases your winnings. Eating a poisonous mushroom decreases your winnings. Your net winnings are used to calculate your bonus.
