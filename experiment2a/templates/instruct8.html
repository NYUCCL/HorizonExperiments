Instructions

Your work will be a great step forward for science! Unfortunately, you have been out longer than expected and have run out of food. To keep your strength up as you hike back to camp, you'll have to eat some of the mushrooms you come across. You know that both species are sometimes healthy and sometimes poisonous, but you don't know how often mushroom in each species are healthy, and how often they are poisonous.

As you hike to camp, you will see mushrooms one at a time in the center of the screen, like this:

When you see a mushroom, press the UP arrow to eat the mushroom or the DOWN arrow to avoid the mushroom. In the upper left corner of the screen, you will see your current ENERGY. Your energy goes up by +5 point when you eat a healthy mushroom and down by -5 points when you eat an unhealthy mushroom. When you avoid a mushroom your energy won't change, and you won't find out whether it was poisonous or not.

Your avatar shows your energy visually. As you gain or lose energy, its expression will change:

Whenever you eat or avoid a mushroom, an observation circle will be added to the species. If you avoid a mushroom a gray circle like the ones you saw earlier will appear. If you eat a healthy mushroom a green circle will appear, and if you eat a poisonous mushroom a red circle will appear. The observation circles are sorted by type, with gray circles first, then red, then green.

When you're ready to continue, press Next.
