Instructions

In the second phase, you have to hike back to your research center. Along the way your goal is to learn which of the area's mushroom species are healthy and which are poisonous - by eating them!

When you see a mushroom on your hike home, its species will be highlighted on the display. Click "eat" to eat the mushroom and discover whether it is healthy or poisonous, or click "avoid" to avoid it.

Note that you can only learn whether a mushroom is healthy or poisonous by eating it. If you choose to avoid a mushroom, you won't find out whether it was healthy.

Important: Each mushroom species is sometimes healthy and sometimes poisonous. You can only learn more about a mushroom by eating it.
