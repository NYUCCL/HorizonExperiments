Instructions

In the second phase, you have to hike back to your research center. However, you have run out of food and so will need to eat some of the mushrooms you come across.

From your research, you know that all species are sometimes healthy and sometimes poisonous. However, you do not know how often mushrooms in each species are healthy or poisonous. Thus, you will want to try to learn as you go in order to keep your energy levels high.

Important: Mushrooms are sometimes healthy and sometimes poisonous. You have to learn which to eat so that you can get home safely.
