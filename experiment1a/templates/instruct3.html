Instructions

Species vary in how often they are poisonous, from almost always healthy to almost always poisonous and everywhere in between.

Within each patch, you will choose whether to eat the mushrooms one at a time. Click "eat" to eat a mushroom and discover whether it is healthy or poisonous, or click "avoid" to avoid it.

Note that you can only learn whether a mushroom is healthy or poisonous by eating it. If you choose to avoid a mushroom, you won't find out whether it was healthy.

Important: Most mushroom species are sometimes healthy and sometimes poisonous. You can only learn about a mushroom by eating it.
