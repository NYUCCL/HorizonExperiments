Instructions

To help you in the task, the mushrooms in a patch will be sorted into groups as you eat or avoid them. Whenever you eat or avoid a mushroom, one of the circular outlines will be filled in and the mushroom will be sorted into a group.

If you eat a healthy mushroom, the circle will become green, and if you eat a poisonous mushroom the circle will become red. If you avoid a mushroom, the circle will become gray, indicating that you do not know whether the mushroom was healthy or poisonous.

Important: A record of your past decisions to eat or avoid mushrooms is displayed in the mushroom groupings on screen (red=poison, green=healthy, and gray=avoided).
