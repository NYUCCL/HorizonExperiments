Instructions

As you hike, you will encounter patches of mushrooms one at a time. Each patch contains a single mushroom species, which is found only in that patch.

Some patches are big and have a lot of mushrooms, while some are small and have few mushrooms. The mushrooms in a patch are shown as a group of circular outlines to the right of the mushroom species icon.

Important: Each species occurs in a single patch. Some species patches have more mushrooms than others.
