Instructions - Payment and Bonus

When you eat a mushroom that is healthy, you will earn two cents ($0.02). When you eat a mushroom that is poisonous, you will lose two cents (-$0.02). If you decide to avoid a mushroom, you won't increase or decrease your winnings.

To maximize your winnings, you should try to eat as many healthy mushrooms as you can, while trying to avoid poisonous mushrooms. You will begin the game with twenty-five cents ($0.25).

At the end of the experiment, your bonus will equal your total winnings.

Important: Eating a mushroom that is healthy increases your winnings. Eating a poisonous mushroom decreases your winnings. Your winnings at the end of the game are used to calculate your bonus.
