Instructions

In this experiment, you will play a simple video game about mushrooms.

You play the role of a mushroom forager exploring the forest and encountering unknown species of mushrooms. As you hike, your goal is to eat as many healthy mushrooms as possible while avoiding poisonous mushrooms.

You will encounter patches of mushrooms one at a time. Each patch contains a single mushroom species, which is found only in that patch.

Some patches are big and have a lot of mushrooms, while some are small and have few mushrooms. The mushrooms in a patch are shown as a group of circular outlines.
