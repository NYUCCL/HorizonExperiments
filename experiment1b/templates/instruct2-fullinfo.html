Instructions

Within each patch, you will choose whether to eat the mushrooms one at a time. Click "eat" to eat a mushroom, or click "don't eat" to not eat it.

As you choose whether or not to eat mushrooms, they will be sorted into groups and change color to record your progress. In addition, your bonus for the experiment will go up or down based on your actions.

If you eat a healthy mushroom, the circle will become a green, upward triangle and you will gain $0.02 . If you eat a poisonous mushroom the circle will become a red, downward triangle and you will lose $0.02. If you don't eat a mushroom, you will instead put it in your mushroom-testing kit, which will tell you whether it would have been healthy or poisonous. Tested healthy mushrooms will turn grayish green, while tested poisonous mushrooms will turn grayish red. You will not gain or lose money when you test a mushroom.

Your bonus starts at $0.25, and will be displayed on screen throughout the experiment.
