Instructions

In the first part of your hike through each environment, you will simply record the different mushrooms you observe and learn about how frequently the different species occur.

Each mushroom you encounter will be recorded as a small, white circle above the correct species icon. Each circle means that you found one mushroom of that species. The more circles that appear above a mushroom, the more common it is as a species. At the end of the first part of the environment you will submit a short field-report on your findings about mushroom frequency.
