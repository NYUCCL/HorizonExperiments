Instructions

In this experiment, you will play a simple video game about mushrooms.

You play the role of a mushroom forager exploring the forest and encountering unknown species of mushrooms. As you hike, your goal is to eat as many healthy mushrooms as possible while avoiding poisonous mushrooms .

You will explore four different environments, each with their own species of mushrooms. Each environment is home to four mushrooms species, found nowhere else in the world. Your hike through each environment will be split into two stages.
