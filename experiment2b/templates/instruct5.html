Instructions

All mushroom species you will encounter fall into two types. Some species are usually healthy -- 2/3 of individual mushrooms from these species are healthy, and only 1/3 are poisonous. Some species are usually poisonous -- only 1/3 of individual mushrooms from these species are healthy, while 2/3 are poisonous. These two types of species occur equally often, though some environments may have more mostly-healthy species than others. There is no way to tell what type a species is by its appearance.

Note: this does not mean that for each mushroom patch you encounter, exactly 2/3 will be healthy or exactly 2/3 will be poisonous. Rather, it means that over the long run, mushrooms from any one species are on average about 2/3 healthy or about 2/3 poisonous.

And remember: your goal isn't to figure out perfectly whether each species is the "usually healthy" type or the "usually poisonous" type, but to maximize your bonus!
