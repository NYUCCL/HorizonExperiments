Instructions

When you encounter a mushroom, its species will be highlighted and a box will expand to show a record of your experiences with that species. As you choose whether or not to eat mushrooms, they will be sorted into groups based on the outcome. In addition, your bonus for the experiment will go up or down based on your actions.

If you eat a healthy mushroom a green, upward-pointing triangle will appear, and you will gain $0.02. If you eat a poisonous mushroom a red downward-pointing triangle will appear, and you will lose $0.02. If your don't eat a mushroom, you will instead put it in your mushroom-testing kit, which tells you whether it would have been healthy or poisonous. Tested healthy mushrooms will turn grayish green, while tested poisonous mushrooms will turn grayish red. You will not gain or lose money when you test a mushroom.

Your bonus starts at $0.25, and will be displayed on screen throughout the decision-making parts of the experiment.
