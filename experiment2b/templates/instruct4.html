Instructions

When you encounter a mushroom, its species will be highlighted and a box will expand to show a record of your experiences with that species. As you choose whether or not to eat mushrooms, they will be sorted into groups based on the outcome. In addition, your bonus for the experiment will go up or down based on your actions.

If you eat a healthy mushroom a green, upward-pointing triangle will appear, and you will gain $0.02. If you eat a poisonous mushroom a red downward-pointing triangle will appear, and you will lose $0.02. If you don't eat a mushroom, a gray circle will appear, and you will not gain or lose any money.

Your bonus starts at $0.25, and will be displayed on screen throughout the decision-making parts of the experiment.
